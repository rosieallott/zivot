How We Help

zivot's technology means that pensions can work more efficiently for savers

Fewer Stranded or Lost Pensions

Zivot ensures members won't lose track of their deferred auto-enrolment pensions. Technology can identify where small deferred pensions match active pots elsewhere.

Improved Member Outcomes

Comparative analysis can identify members who may be better served in consolidating their pension savings. Efficiencies and value analysis mean reduced costs to the member.

Simplified Pension Transfers

Initiating and tracking transfers, and communicating with the member, are greatly streamlined due to the consistent data created by Zivot's technology.
